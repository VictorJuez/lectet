Shipping information


The shipping options may vary based on the delivery address, the time of purchase and the availability of items.

At the time of processing your purchase, we will inform you via email about the delivery date of your order.

Possible delivery options:

  • Collection eBook - FREE

    The eBook will be send you via email, also you will have the possibility of download from your profile.

  • Collection Paperback / Hardcover:

    • Standard delivery - FREE

      The estimated delivery period is 2-4 business days, depending on the delivery address.

The time periods indicated above will be longer for more remote regions.

Please remember that deliveries are only made on business days.



Order status

You’ll receive order status updates via email.

You can also access this information in your profile.  If you aren’t registered, click on the “Order Information” link in your confirmation email to track it.


Restrictions

At Lectet, we try our hardest to reach all corners of the world. If there are any shipping restrictions for your delivery address, you’ll see this at the time of purchase.


Split orders

Your order may be dispatched from various warehouses. If this happens, we’ll notify you via email to tell you which items you’ll be receiving in each delivery.
