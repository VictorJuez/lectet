Q: Is it necessary to have account to shop on lectect?

For viewing books you don't need a account but you need an account to buy a book.

Q: Can I order order multiple books together?

Yes, you can buy multiple books as long as delivery address is same.

Q: How to order from lectect?

To buy books from Lectet you need to press the buy button and then login and fill informations for delivery and payment.

Q: When can I expect my order to arrive?

Usually your books will arrive within a week from booking day. But if it doesn't we suggest you to contact our customer care.

Q: How do I make an account for letect?

To create an account in our website you need to follow two simple steps. first you need to click the login button in that page you will find a button for sign up and then you need to feel a form.

Q: How to login?

click on the login link on the top right corner of the website then you can fill the form with your login information.
